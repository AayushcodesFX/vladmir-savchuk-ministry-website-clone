STUDENTS



Middle and high school is a time of self-discovery and greater freedom. Mountain Students partners with parents and invites students into something greater than themselves. Students is a community that will challenge them to think about life in a way that provides significance, purpose, and belonging.

Middle and high school can be tough, but there’s no need to go through it alone. Come together with other students from the area on a journey of following Jesus, being a part of a community where you can be real, and becoming a difference maker who makes a positive impact wherever you go.
